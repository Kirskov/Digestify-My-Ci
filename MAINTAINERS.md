# Maintainers

## Roles

**Maintainer**
- Reviews and merges pull requests
- Triages issues and bug reports
- Publishes releases and signs artifacts
- Manages repository settings, secrets, and access
- Sets project direction and priorities

**Contributor**
- Submits pull requests and bug reports
- No access to sensitive resources or release tooling

## Escalated permissions policy

Granting any collaborator escalated permissions (write access, merge approval, access to secrets or release tooling) requires:

1. **Review** — the existing maintainer(s) must review the candidate's prior contributions to the project and their identity
2. **Identity verification** — the candidate must have a verifiable association with a known trusted organization or a demonstrable contribution history under a consistent identity
3. **Explicit approval** — access is only granted after explicit approval by the current maintainer(s), never automatically
4. **Least privilege** — permissions are scoped to what the role requires; no collaborator receives admin access unless strictly necessary

Contributors who have not been through this process have no access to sensitive resources (secrets, release publishing, repository settings).

## Escalated permissions policy

This is a single-maintainer project. No collaborator is granted write access, merge approval, or access to secrets without explicit review and approval by the maintainer. Any future addition of a collaborator with escalated permissions will require a demonstrated contribution history and verified identity before access is granted.
